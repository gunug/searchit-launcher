A cute white 3D bear mascot stands beside a giant Android smartphone.

The smartphone screen is filled with many folders and cluttered app organization.

There are 16+ folders visible, notification badges, and multiple categories scattered everywhere.

The bear scratches its head with a worried expression.

A hand-drawn scribble cloud appears above the bear to represent confusion.

Bright minimal background, soft daylight, clean commercial style.

No humans.

The scene should clearly communicate app organization fatigue.

Korean text area should be left empty for later editing.
